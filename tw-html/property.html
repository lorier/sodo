The Property

SoDo Urbanworks (Formerly City Commerce Business Park) is a multi-tenant property located in the heart of South Seattle’s thriving SoDo neighborhood. Built in 1946, the business park sits on 6.8 acres that includes customer parking and greenspace. Our tenants appreciate the easy access, well-maintained public spaces, and eclectic mix of businesses.

  • Total Rentable Space: 27,882 SF
  • Rental Rate: $0.90 - $1.33 /SF/Month
  • Min. Divisible Space: 4,406 SF
  • Max. Contiguous Space: 6,065 SF
  • Zoning Designation: Industrial General (IG1 U/85)

Benefits

  • Fast Growing Neighborhood
  • Proximity to downtown and transit
  • Easy access to I-5
  • Directly visibility from 1st Ave S
  • Dock and grade-level loading
  • 16’ to 20’ clear height
