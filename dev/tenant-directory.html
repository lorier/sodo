Featured Tenants

Block Wines

As a “house winery” for the Seattle retailer Full Pull Wines, Block Wines creates single-variety wines – from single blocks within single vineyards – to express each small patch of land as transparently as possible.

fullpullwines.com

Kerloo Cellars

From Rhône and Syrah wines to varietals like Malbec and Grenache, Kerloo Cellars makes vineyard-specific wines that showcase the grapes’ unique terroir.

kerloocellars.com

Sleight of Hand Cellars

New to Seattle from the Walla Walla Valley, Sleight of Hand Cellars offers a wide selection of world-class wines from Washington State’s best vineyard sites.

schoonerexact.com

Rotie Cellars

Specializing in wine blends of the Rhône Valley, France, Rotie produces small and focused batches from some of the best and oldest vineyards in Washington State.

rotiecellars.com

Structure Cellars

From humble beginnings in their Ballard basement, Brian and Brandee, the owners and winemakers of Structure Cellars, have created approachable, food friendly, fruit forward and varietally sound wines with an emphasis on old-world style

structurewines.com

Waters Winery

In limited, small-lot bottling batches, Waters Winery makes distinctive red wines in the Walla Walla Valley that reflect the unique character of each varietal and the qualities of the land from which they come.

trwines.com

Schooner Exact

In 2006, Schooner EXACT was created, the brainchild of three friends over a batch of homebrew. The next year, the first beers began to flow to the public through pubs, alehouses and restaurants. The family friendly restaurant in SODO Urbanworks was opened in 2012.

schoonerexact.com
